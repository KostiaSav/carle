How it works

Smartphone app (Android/iOS)

  • Daily quick check-ins
  • CBT-informed self-management library
  • Mood journaling
  • ≥75% annual adherence rates

Predictive algorithm

  • Learns personal baseline in 28 days
  • Flags events 3–10 days before crisis
  • Email notification to key worker

Digital triage

  • Alerts routed to care teams
  • Personalised support plans

Co-designed at every stage by patients, clinicians, researchers and software engineers to work for people and systems.
